Thank you for visiting Daves Pro Wash. My name is David and my friends name is David as well. We work in Sedalia, Missouri and our company was founded in 2021.

We had the idea pop into our heads when we needed money to live. The two Davids put their heads together and started a comany called Daves Pro Wash. I am 2020 graduate of the State Fair Community College and my friend is a hard worker who put in a lot of physical labor.

We believe that all houses and personal properties should be clean and welcoming. Sedalia is a small growing town what still has a lot of old fashioned buildings and lots of room for the city to grow. Sedalia was established in 1860 and there are lots of historical things that are still persistant here. We have a mission to clean up peoples houses, driveways. sidewalks you name it.
